Ph.D. Fellow in Computer Science

University of Hamburg

Biography

I am a research associate at University of Hamburg. My research interests include Deep Reinforcement Learning, Cognetive Robotics, and Natural Language Processing.

I am passionate about deep learning and AI with a focus on deep reinforcement learning and its applications in robotics. My goal is to build a reinforcement learning model for robots to safely interact in a domestic environment using natural language instructions in a human-robot interaction scenario.
